About Me

Get to know the developer behind the code

Hi, I'm Ali, a 14-year-old self-taught developer with a passion for creating things that live on the internet. My journey in web development started 4 years ago when I discovered W3Schools and began learning HTML and CSS on my mobile phone, before I even had a laptop!

What began as curiosity quickly turned into a passion as I fell in love with the process of building websites and seeing my code come to life. I've since expanded my skills to include Python (Currently learning.), cybersecurity (Currently learning.) and Lua. I am always eager to learn new technologies and improve my craft.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring cybersecurity concepts or learning other programming languages. I believe in using technology to solve real-world problems and make a positive impact.

Despite my young age, I take my work seriously and I am constantly pushing myself to grow as a developer. Every project is an opportunity to learn something new and improve my skills.
